Recent Work

These are some of the projects I've worked on.

Volt Vision

This was a crowdsourced project that raised over 1000$. My team and I worked on a wearable that could interpret eye motion and output the direction a user was looking to control any other device through bluetooth. The primary use case was to enable disabled individuals to control wheelchairs, toys, or any other application.

Phone Controlled Robot

This project was to develop a wheel driven robot with a mounted ping-pong ball launcher that could be controlled via an app I developed. This robot was operated by a microcontroller that was connected to my phone app.
